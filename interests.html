Writing

I do a lot of journaling and one of my goals in life is to write a novel. In the past year, I've written over 265,000 words in an online journal.

That doesn't count any creative writing I've done. I find that journaling and writing is a positive habit for my mental health.

Crochet

I love crocheting. I find it to be a somewhat mindful practice and relaxes me.

Recently, I've started crocheting a "plarn" sleeping mat to donate to someone who is homeless or a homeless shelter.

Plarn is made by cutting plastic bags or garbage bags into loops and making a ball by linking them together
